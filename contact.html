My Approach to Initial Conversations

Every situation is different, so I prefer to start by understanding your specific circumstances before suggesting any particular approach. Sometimes a single conversation provides exactly the clarity needed; other times, ongoing collaborative support through a transition period is more helpful.

The initial conversation helps us both understand whether working together makes sense for your situation.

People Often Get In Touch When They're:

  • Considering a significant career change but feeling unsure about the best approach to take
  • Feeling caught between staying in their current role or making a move to something different
  • Clear about wanting change but uncertain about what direction would be most fulfilling
  • Facing a transition they know is right but feeling apprehensive about taking the next steps
  • Looking for structured support and guidance while navigating a complex career change
  • Wanting to think through their options with someone who understands senior-level transitions
  • Needing clarity on how to balance continued impact with personal fulfilment
  • Ready to design their next chapter but want collaborative support in the planning
