About Paul Thomas

Transformation specialist supporting senior leaders through significant transitions

Supporting Senior Leaders Through Change

I work with senior leaders who are navigating significant career transitions and organisational changes. My approach combines proven change methodologies with practical, supportive guidance tailored to the unique challenges that come with leadership responsibility.

Whether you're considering a major career shift, designing your next chapter, or working through complex leadership challenges, I provide the space, frameworks, and honest perspective to help you move forward with clarity and confidence.

Background & Experience

  • MA in Change - specialist qualification in transformation and organisational development
  • Former Royal Navy officer - bringing structured, strategic thinking to complex challenges
  • Extensive consultancy experience across public and private sector organisations
  • International programme delivery including advanced communication training (NLP Master Practitioner)
  • Senior leadership programmes - design and delivery of executive development initiatives
  • Collaborative partnership with senior academic leaders (identity protected for confidentiality)

My Approach to Transitions

I believe that the best transitions happen when people have the space to think clearly, explore their options thoroughly, and move forward with genuine confidence rather than rushed decisions.

My role is to provide that thinking space, along with:

  • Practical frameworks for working through complex decisions
  • Honest perspective when it's helpful and welcome
  • Structured support through the uncertainties that come with change
  • Accountability for the actions that matter most
  • Confidential space to think through sensitive career and leadership issues

What I Particularly Enjoy

I find it most rewarding to help senior leaders with:

  • Thinking through complex career decisions and weighing up different pathways
  • Navigating the identity shifts that come with major role changes
  • Designing their next chapter in a way that aligns with their values and long-term goals
  • Working through the practical challenges and logistics of significant transitions
  • Building confidence for moving into unfamiliar or challenging territory
  • Balancing continued impact and influence with personal fulfilment and satisfaction

The NAVIGATE™ Methodology

Over the years, I've developed a comprehensive framework for supporting people through transitions that combines structured planning with space for reflection and exploration.

The NAVIGATE™ approach ensures that changes are both:

  • Well thought through - using proven frameworks and strategic thinking
  • Personally meaningful - aligned with your values, goals, and authentic aspirations

This methodology forms the foundation of the assessment tools and resources available on this site, as well as the collaborative work I do with individual leaders.

Working Together

Every situation is different, so I prefer to start with a conversation about your specific circumstances before suggesting any particular approach or structure.

Sometimes:

  • A single strategic conversation provides the clarity and confidence needed
  • A few sessions over several weeks helps work through a specific decision
  • Ongoing support through a longer transition period is most valuable

The most important thing is that the approach feels right for your situation and provides genuinely helpful support for your transition journey.
