About Paul Thomas

From 'Educational Failure' to Supporting Senior Academics Worldwide

At 16, I was labelled an "educational failure." Many decades later, I've coached senior leaders and supported organisations worldwide to grow and succeed.

My journey—from engineering apprentice to Lieutenant in the Royal Navy, to trusted coach and consultant—has been shaped by resilience, curiosity and lifelong learning.

Since the early 1990s, I've been privileged to support academic professionals in Higher Education, helping them navigate the unique challenges and opportunities of senior leadership in complex institutional environments.

Professional Journey

Royal Navy
Lieutenant in the Royal Navy where I discovered my passion for learning and development, understanding how to support people through complex transitions.
Kellogg's
Training and Development Manager for a greenfield site which introduced pioneering team working to the UK, learning organisational transformation from the ground up.
UK Higher Education
Coached and supported senior managers from nearly 50 UK universities who were managing complex organisational change projects, understanding the unique pressures of academic leadership.
International Consultancy
Delivered MBA units in Saudi Arabia and worked with CEOs in the Czech Republic, bringing evidence-based leadership development to diverse cultural contexts.

My Approach to Supporting Academics

I bring a people-first approach to everything I do—listening carefully, supporting consistently, and challenging appropriately.

Having worked extensively within Higher Education, I understand the unique culture, pressures, and opportunities that define academic careers. I create space for clarity, confidence and meaningful progress using evidence-based frameworks that respect both rigorous thinking and personal development.

Whether you're exploring options beyond traditional academia, stepping into senior leadership roles, or seeking to enhance your impact within Higher Education, my role is to provide the strategic thinking partner you need to navigate these important decisions with confidence.

Academic Expertise

My work with senior academics encompasses several key areas:

Career Transition
Supporting academics exploring opportunities beyond traditional university roles, from industry consulting to policy work and entrepreneurship.
Leadership Development
Helping academics step into senior institutional roles with confidence, from Head of Department to Pro-Vice-Chancellor positions.
Academic Resilience
Building sustainable practices for managing the unique pressures of academic life whilst maintaining personal wellbeing and professional effectiveness.
Strategic Thinking
Applying evidence-based frameworks to complex career decisions, helping academics think strategically about their next chapter.
