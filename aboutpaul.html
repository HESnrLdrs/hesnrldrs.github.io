My Journey

I've spent over three decades working within Higher Education, experiencing firsthand the unique challenges and opportunities that define academic careers. From early-career lecturer to senior leadership positions, I understand the complex decisions that senior academics face when considering their next career moves.

My own career transitions taught me that change in academia requires both strategic thinking and emotional intelligence. It's not just about moving from one role to another—it's about understanding your values, leveraging your expertise, and creating a path that aligns with your personal and professional aspirations.

Why I Focus on Senior Academics

Senior academics bring extraordinary expertise to any transition. You've built careers on critical thinking, research excellence, and knowledge creation. However, the academic environment can sometimes limit our perspective on what's possible beyond traditional academic roles.

I work with senior academics because I understand the unique value you bring to any organization, and the specific challenges you face when considering change. Whether you're looking at leadership roles within academia, transitioning to industry, or exploring consulting opportunities, your experience deserves thoughtful, strategic guidance.

My Approach

My coaching is grounded in evidence-based practice and designed specifically for the academic mindset. I combine practical career strategy with reflective exploration, helping you gain clarity on your goals while developing concrete action plans to achieve them.

Every coaching relationship is unique. Some clients need help identifying their transferable skills, others require support in building confidence for new challenges, and many benefit from strategic networking guidance. Whatever your specific needs, we'll work together to create a tailored approach that respects your experience while opening doors to new possibilities.
