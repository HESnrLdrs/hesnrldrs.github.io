Professional Coaching

Evidence-based support for senior academics navigating transformation

I work with senior academics who are ready to make meaningful changes in their careers and lives. Whether you're exploring opportunities beyond traditional academia, stepping into leadership roles, or seeking to enhance your impact within Higher Education, my coaching provides the strategic support you need.

With over three decades of experience supporting academic professionals, I understand the unique challenges you face and the opportunities available to you. My approach combines evidence-based frameworks with practical, actionable strategies tailored to your specific situation.

My Coaching Approach

🎯

Strategic Thinking

How We Work Together

1

Discovery & Assessment

We begin with a comprehensive exploration of your current situation, goals, and challenges. This includes structured assessments and reflective exercises.

2

Strategic Planning

Together, we develop a clear roadmap that outlines your objectives, identifies potential obstacles, and creates actionable steps toward your goals.

As you take action, I provide ongoing support, accountability, and guidance. We adjust the plan as needed based on your progress and changing circumstances.

Reflection & Refinement
