Development Workbooks

Comprehensive resources for strategic career development and transformation

Our development workbooks provide in-depth, structured approaches to implementing insights from your assessment results. Each workbook contains evidence-based frameworks, practical exercises, and strategic planning tools designed specifically for senior academics.

How to Use These Workbooks

1. Complete the Assessment First

Begin with the relevant assessment tool to understand your current situation and receive personalised insights that will guide your workbook journey.

2. Work Through Systematically

Each workbook is designed as a structured programme. Set aside dedicated time to engage meaningfully with the exercises and reflection prompts.

3. Apply and Implement

Use the strategic planning tools and action templates to translate insights into concrete steps and sustainable changes in your professional life.

4. What would you really find helpful?

If there's a workbook topic that's missing from your development toolkit, let's have a conversation about how we can work together to create exactly what you need.
