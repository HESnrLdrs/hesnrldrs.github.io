Transformation Workbooks

Practical, research-based workbooks designed to guide senior academics through career transitions and personal development.

How to Use These Workbooks

1. Choose Your Focus

Select the workbook that best matches your current situation or development goals. Each workbook is designed to work independently.

2. Work Through Systematically

Follow the structured exercises and reflection prompts. Allow time for thoughtful consideration - transformation takes time.

3. Apply and Reflect

Implement the insights and strategies in your daily work. Regular reflection deepens the learning and solidifies change.

4. What would you really find helpful?

If there's an assessment or workbook that's missing from your toolkit, let's have a conversation about how we can work together to create exactly what you need.
